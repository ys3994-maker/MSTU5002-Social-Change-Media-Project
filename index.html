Project Info
Scroll Free

An interactive digital story exploring phone addiction and the quiet moments we lose to the scroll — and how to gently reclaim them.

Title

Scroll Free: An Interactive Story About You and Your Phone

Description

A scroll-based interactive comic story that walks users through a day of phone addiction, reveals the psychological trap behind it, and invites them to build their own phone-free plan — ending with a personal habit tracker.

Intended Audience

Young adults aged 16–30 who recognise their own compulsive scrolling habits but haven't found a concrete way to change them.

Plan for Dissemination

Share via social media link, display during the final class showcase, embed as a QR code poster in school common areas, and distribute via class group chat.

Made with ✨ for a world with less scrolling and more presence.
